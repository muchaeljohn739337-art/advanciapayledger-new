## üí° TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Email Outreach Best Practices
1. **Personalize**: Spend 10-15 min researching each partner
2. **Be Concise**: Keep emails under 150 words
3. **Clear CTA**: Always include specific next step (call, meeting)
4. **Timing**: Send Tue-Thu, 9-11am in recipient's timezone
5. **Follow-Up**: 7 days, then 14 days, then close loop

### Meeting Preparation
1. **Research**: Know their business, recent news, pain points
2. **Materials**: Have one-pager and ROI calculator ready
3. **Agenda**: Send agenda 24 hours before call
4. **Questions**: Prepare 5-10 discovery questions
5. **Follow-Up**: Send recap within 24 hours of meeting

### Partnership Negotiation
1. **Start High**: Begin with ambitious terms, negotiate down
2. **Exclusivity**: Limit to 6-12 months max with performance exit
3. **Pilot First**: Propose 90-day pilot before full commitment
4. **Win-Win**: Focus on mutual value, not just your needs
5. **Legal Review**: Have attorney review before signing
