# Advancia Pay Ledger Style Guide

**Version 1.0 | January 2026**

## Brand Overview

Advancia Pay Ledger is a healthcare fintech platform that bridges traditional and cryptocurrency payments for healthcare facilities. We enable healthcare providers to accept both fiat and crypto payments while maintaining HIPAA and PCI-DSS compliance.

**Mission**: Modernize healthcare payments with secure, flexible payment processing that meets facilities where they are.

**Vision**: Become the leading payment infrastructure for healthcare, making cryptocurrency adoption seamless and compliant.

---

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Core Voice Attributes

**Professional Yet Approachable**
- We're experts in fintech and healthcare, but we speak plainly
- Avoid jargon unless speaking to technical audiences
- Confidence without arrogance

**Innovation-Driven**
- Forward-thinking about payment technology
- Emphasize cutting-edge solutions with proven reliability
- Balance "new" with "trustworthy"

**Security-Focused**
- Compliance and security are non-negotiable
- Transparency about our safeguards builds trust
- Never downplay regulatory requirements

**Execution-Oriented**
- Direct and clear communication
- Focus on results and metrics
- Action over theory

### Tone by Audience

**Healthcare Facilities (Primary Customers)**
- Professional and reassuring
- Focus on operational benefits (reduced fees, faster settlements, compliance ease)
- Use specific examples: "Accept Bitcoin payments while maintaining HIPAA compliance"
- Avoid: Crypto hype, technical blockchain jargon

**Investors**
- Data-driven and strategic
- Highlight traction metrics (MRR, growth rate, customer count)
- Clear unit economics and scalability story
- Professional, confident tone with backup evidence

**Patients/End Users**
- Simple and helpful
- Focus on payment flexibility and ease of use
- Never mention blockchain/crypto complexity
- "Pay your way" messaging

**Technical Partners/Developers**
- Detailed and precise
- Technical accuracy is paramount
- API documentation should be comprehensive
- Use proper technical terminology

## Writing Guidelines

### General Rules

**Be Direct**
- ✅ "Advancia processes crypto payments for healthcare facilities"
- ❌ "Advancia is revolutionizing the paradigm of healthcare payment ecosystems"

**Use Active Voice**
- ✅ "We process payments in minutes"
- ❌ "Payments are processed in minutes"

**Quantify When Possible**
- ✅ "24 healthcare facilities trust Advancia"
- ❌ "Many healthcare facilities trust Advancia"

**Avoid Unnecessary Jargon**
- ✅ "Accept cryptocurrency payments"
- ❌ "Leverage distributed ledger technology for value transfer"

### Word Choice

**Preferred Terms:**
- Healthcare facility (not hospital, clinic unless specific)
- Platform (not app, tool, solution)
- Cryptocurrency / crypto (not blockchain, unless technical context)
- Payment processing (not transactions, unless specific)
- Compliance (not regulatory adherence)

**Avoid:**
- Disruptive, revolutionary, paradigm shift (overused in fintech)
- Seamless (unless truly demonstrable)
- Next-gen, cutting-edge (show don't tell)
- Game-changer, innovative (provide evidence instead)

### Numbers & Metrics

**Always Spell Out:**
- One through nine in body text
- Numbers at start of sentences

**Use Numerals For:**
- 10 and above
- Percentages (42%)
- Currency ($247K, $1.5M)
- Technical specs (256-bit encryption)

**Rounding:**
- MRR: Round to nearest thousand ($247K not $246,891)
- Growth rates: One decimal place (42.3% not 42.28%)
- Large numbers: Use K/M notation ($1.5M not $1,500,000)

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- **Deep Blue**: #1A365D (Trust, professionalism, healthcare)
- **Vibrant Teal**: #0891B2 (Innovation, technology, forward-thinking)

**Secondary Colors:**
- **Slate Gray**: #475569 (Professional, neutral)
- **White**: #FFFFFF (Clean, modern)

**Accent Colors:**
- **Success Green**: #059669 (Positive actions, confirmations)
- **Warning Orange**: #EA580C (Alerts, important notices)
- **Error Red**: #DC2626 (Errors, critical alerts)

**Usage:**
- Primary CTA buttons: Vibrant Teal
- Headers and navigation: Deep Blue
- Body text: Slate Gray on White
- Never use more than 3 colors in one design element

### Typography

**Headings:**
- Font: Inter Bold or Poppins Bold
- Sizes: H1 (48px), H2 (36px), H3 (24px), H4 (20px)
- Color: Deep Blue (#1A365D)

**Body Text:**
- Font: Inter Regular or Open Sans
- Size: 16px (desktop), 14px (mobile)
- Line height: 1.6
- Color: Slate Gray (#475569)

**Technical/Code:**
- Font: JetBrains Mono or Fira Code
- Size: 14px
- Background: Light gray (#F1F5F9)

### Logo Usage

**Clear Space:**
- Minimum clear space around logo: Equal to height of the "A" in Advancia
- Never place logo where it touches other elements

**Minimum Size:**
- Digital: 120px width
- Print: 1 inch width

**Don'ts:**
- Don't rotate or skew the logo
- Don't change logo colors (use provided versions)
- Don't add effects (shadows, outlines, gradients)
- Don't place on busy backgrounds

### Imagery Guidelines

**Photography Style:**
- Clean, professional healthcare settings
- Diverse patients and healthcare workers
- Natural lighting preferred
- Avoid stock photo clichés (stethoscope on keyboard, etc.)

**Icons:**
- Line style, not filled
- 2px stroke weight
- Rounded corners (2px radius)
- Consistent sizing within contexts

## Content Types

### Website Copy

**Homepage:**
- Hero: Clear value prop in ≤10 words
- Sub-hero: Expand on value prop in 1-2 sentences
- Social proof: Customer count, MRR, or notable clients
- CTA: Specific action ("Schedule Demo", not "Learn More")

**Product Pages:**
- Lead with customer benefit, not feature
- Use specific examples
- Include screenshots/demos
- End with clear next step

### Investor Materials

**Pitch Deck:**
- 10-15 slides maximum
- Lead with traction slide
- Clear ask and use of funds
- Team credentials prominent

**Email Outreach:**
- Subject: Specific and relevant
- Body: ≤150 words
- One clear ask
- Professional signature with title

**Financial Models:**
- Conservative assumptions clearly stated
- Multiple scenarios (base, optimistic, conservative)
- Clear drivers and unit economics
- Updated monthly

### Marketing Content

**Blog Posts:**
- Headline: Clear benefit or insight
- Length: 800-1,200 words
- Include data/examples
- Strong CTA at end

**Social Media:**
- LinkedIn: Professional insights, company updates, thought leadership
- Twitter: Industry news, quick tips, engagement
- Length: Keep to platform best practices
- Always include relevant visual

**Case Studies:**
- Customer name (with permission)
- Problem → Solution → Results format
- Quantify results when possible
- Quote from customer contact

## Compliance & Legal

### Required Disclaimers

**Investment Materials:**
"This presentation is for informational purposes only and does not constitute an offer to sell or solicitation of an offer to buy securities."

**Cryptocurrency:**
"Cryptocurrency investments carry risks. Past performance does not indicate future results. Consult appropriate professionals before making investment decisions."

**Healthcare Data:**
All materials must comply with HIPAA privacy requirements. Never include:
- Patient names or identifiable information
- Specific facility names without written permission
- Protected health information (PHI) of any kind

### Regulatory Language

**When Discussing Compliance:**
- ✅ "HIPAA and PCI-DSS compliant"
- ❌ "We handle HIPAA stuff"

**When Discussing Crypto:**
- ✅ "Cryptocurrency payment processing"
- ❌ Avoid: "Crypto trading", "investment platform", "returns"

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Email Communications

**Subject Lines:**
- ≤50 characters
- Specific and actionable
- Avoid: ALL CAPS, excessive punctuation!!!, clickbait

**Structure:**
- Greeting (personalized when possible)
- 2-3 short paragraphs max
- Clear call-to-action
- Professional signature

**Signature:**
```
[Name]
[Title], Advancia Pay Ledger
[Email] | [Phone]
advanciapayledger.com
```

### Sales Materials

**Discovery Calls:**
- Focus on listening first
- Ask about current payment processing pain points
- Quantify their costs/challenges
- Position Advancia as solution to their specific needs

**Demos:**
- Customize to prospect's use case
- Show actual platform, not slides
- Emphasize compliance and security features
- End with clear next steps

**Proposals:**
- Executive summary ≤1 page
- Pricing clearly broken out
- Implementation timeline
- Support/onboarding details

## Don'ts - Critical Guidelines

### Never Mention to End Users:
- AI technology or automation
- Claude or any AI tools
- "Algorithm" or "machine learning"
- Present platform as traditional software with human support

### Avoid in All External Comms:
- Making claims about "security" without specifics
- Promising specific returns or financial outcomes
- Comparing directly to competitors by name
- Using patient data or PHI in examples
