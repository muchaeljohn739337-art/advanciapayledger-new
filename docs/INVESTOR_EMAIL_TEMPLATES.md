## USAGE TIPS

**Personalization checklist before sending:**
1. Research the investor's recent investments and mention one specifically
2. Check their LinkedIn/Twitter for recent posts—reference if relevant
3. Adjust technical depth based on their background (healthcare vs fintech vs AI)
4. If warm intro, reference the mutual connection prominently
5. Keep to 150 words or less for cold outreach

**Subject line variants:**
- "$200B market + 80% cost reduction = opportunity"
- "The Stripe of healthcare, but with crypto"
- "First-mover in crypto healthcare infrastructure"
- "25 AI agents solving healthcare operations"

**Best practices:**
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am or 2-4pm in their timezone
- Follow up after 5-7 days if no response
- Attach the one-pager PDF to follow-ups, not initial cold emails
- Always end with a specific ask (call availability, feedback, intro to partner)
